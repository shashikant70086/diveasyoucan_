"# DIVE AS YOU CAN

A deep-sea diving game where you navigate the Mariana Trench to survive and collect treasure.

## About

DIVE AS YOU CAN is a browser-based arcade game where you control a submarine diving deeper into the ocean. Collect coins and oxygen tanks while avoiding deadly sea creatures. The deeper you go, the narrower the trench becomes—test your reflexes and strategy!

## How to Play

### Controls
- **W / ↑** - Move Up
- **S / ↓** - Move Down  
- **A / ←** - Move Left
- **D / →** - Move Right
- **Mouse** - Aim your torch/light

### Game Mechanics
- **Oxygen**: Depletes as you dive deeper. Refill by resurfacing or collecting oxygen tanks.
- **Health**: Takes damage from enemy encounters. Health cannot be recovered.
- **Coins**: Collect pearls/coins for points.
- **Enemies**: 
  - 🦈 Sharks (e1) - Rare, home in from the walls
  - 🐟 Fish (e2) - Common, bob around and patrol walls
  - 🪼 Jellyfish (e3) - Medium frequency, high damage

### Objective
Survive as deep as possible while managing your oxygen and health. Game over when oxygen or health reaches zero.

## Getting Started

1. Open `index.html` in a web browser
2. Click to start the game
3. Use WASD or arrow keys to navigate
4. Aim the light with your mouse
5. Dive deeper and beat your high score!

## Game Features
- Physics-based movement with gravity and friction
- Dynamic lighting system (cone + soft glow)
- Procedurally spawned items at increasing depth
- Screen shake and visual feedback on damage
- Real-time health and oxygen meters
- Depth tracking

## Assets
Game includes custom sprite assets for:
- Player submarine/diver
- Pearl/coin collectible
- Oxygen tank
- Three enemy types (shark, fish, jellyfish)
